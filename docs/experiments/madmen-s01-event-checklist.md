## Expected Plotlines — Mad Men S01

Ground truth for plotline precision/recall. Based on domain knowledge of the show, not LLM output.

1. **Don: Identity** — Don hides his true identity (Dick Whitman), past surfaces through encounters and flashbacks. Character-led.
2. **Don: Marriage** — Don and Betty's deteriorating marriage, his infidelity, her isolation. Character-led.
3. **Peggy: Career** — from secretary to copywriter, navigating a world that doesn't take women seriously. Character-led.
4. **Pete: Ambition** — climbing the corporate ladder, scheming, rivalries with Don and colleagues. Character-led.
5. **Betty: Isolation** — suburban life, therapy, relationship with neighbors, growing awareness of Don's distance. Character-led.
6. **Joan: Office Power** — navigating the agency as a woman with authority but no title. Could be runner.
7. **Roger: Personal Life** — affair with Joan, drinking, relationship with Don. Could be runner.

**Note:** some events across multiple character plotlines may form an institutional dynamic (agency campaigns, office politics). This is not a required plotline — but if the model suggests one, check whether it adds analytical value or just repackages events already covered by character plotlines.
