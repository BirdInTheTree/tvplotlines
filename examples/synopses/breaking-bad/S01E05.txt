Walt and Skyler attend Elliott Schwartz's birthday party, where Walt reunites with his former business partner Elliott, now CEO of Gray Matter Technologies, and Elliott's wife Gretchen, Walt's former fiancée. Elliott offers Walt a job at Gray Matter after reminiscing about their graduate school days together. Walt realizes Skyler must have revealed his cancer diagnosis to Elliott and angrily declines the offer, viewing it as charity rather than genuine opportunity. Jesse grows increasingly frustrated with his inability to find legitimate employment and attempts to cook meth with his friend Badger using Walt's recipe, but the batch fails without Walt's expertise. Walt Jr. gets caught by police trying to buy beer underage and calls his uncle Hank instead of his father for help. Hank picks up Walt Jr. and lectures him about not turning to Walt first, highlighting the growing distance between father and son. Walt's family stages an intervention to pressure him into accepting chemotherapy treatment. Walt refuses, insisting he needs the autonomy to make his own medical decisions and doesn't want to spend his remaining time suffering through treatment side effects. Skyler, Hank, Marie, and Walt Jr. all plead with him to reconsider, but Walt remains firm about maintaining control over his fate. The next morning, Walt appears to relent and tells Skyler he will accept Elliott and Gretchen's financial assistance for his cancer treatment. When Gretchen calls to discuss the arrangement, Walt lies and tells her the help is no longer necessary because his insurance will cover the costs after all. Walt then drives to Jesse's house and asks to resume their methamphetamine partnership, having decided to fund his treatment through drug money rather than accept what he perceives as charity from his former colleagues.
