Walt and Jesse return to Albuquerque with two bodies in their RV, but discover that Krazy-8 has disappeared from where they left him. While driving home from work, Walt finds a barely-conscious Krazy-8 wandering through Jesse's neighborhood and makes the decision to bring him back to Jesse's house. Walt restrains Krazy-8 with a bicycle lock in the basement, creating a hostage situation he struggles to resolve. Instead of killing Krazy-8 immediately, Walt procrastinates by providing him with food, water, and basic necessities, unable to commit to murder despite the obvious danger. At a routine ultrasound appointment, Skyler confronts Walt about his suspicious recent behavior, specifically questioning him about his mysterious phone call with Jesse. Walt fabricates a cover story, telling Skyler that Jesse is his marijuana dealer, hoping this explanation will satisfy her concerns. Skyler takes matters into her own hands and visits Jesse directly at his house, threatening to call her DEA agent brother-in-law Hank if Jesse continues selling marijuana to Walt. Jesse, intimidated by the threat, agrees to her demands. Back at Jesse's house, Jesse attempts to dispose of Emilio's corpse using hydrofluoric acid as Walt instructed, but ignores Walt's specific recommendation to use a plastic container. Jesse instead uses his upstairs bathtub for the acid dissolution, resulting in a catastrophic chemical reaction that eats through the bathtub and floor. The acid creates a massive hole in the bathroom floor, spilling dissolved body parts throughout the house and creating an enormous cleanup problem. Meanwhile, two children playing in the desert area where Walt and Jesse had previously parked their RV discover a gas mask that the pair accidentally left behind, creating potential evidence that could link them to their criminal activities.
