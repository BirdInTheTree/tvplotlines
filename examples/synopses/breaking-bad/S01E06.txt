Walt begins his first chemotherapy session at the hospital, marking the start of his cancer treatment. He and Jesse continue their partnership with Walt handling the cooking while Jesse manages distribution and sales. During a cooking session in the RV, Walt removes his shirt and Jesse notices the radiation therapy marks on his chest, leading Jesse to realize that Walt has lung cancer. This revelation adds a new dynamic to their partnership as Jesse now understands Walt's desperation. Meanwhile, Hank continues investigating the missing chemistry equipment from Walt's high school. He traces the recovered respirator back to the school's chemistry lab and focuses his investigation there. Hank arrests Hugo Archilleya, the school's janitor, falsely accusing him of stealing the equipment. Hugo's previous drug possession record and the small amount of marijuana found in his car make him an easy target, despite his innocence. Walt watches helplessly as an innocent man takes the blame for his crimes. Walt's cancer treatment begins affecting his appearance as his hair starts falling out. Rather than wait for the gradual loss, Walt decides to shave his head completely, symbolically embracing his transformation. When Walt and Jesse sell their first batch of methamphetamine, they discover it only yields $2,600, far less than Walt expected for their high-quality product. Walt realizes they need to significantly increase production to make the financial impact he needs for his family. Jesse's friend Skinny Pete introduces him to Tuco Salamanca, the violent drug lord who has taken over Krazy-8's territory as a distributor. Jesse approaches Tuco to establish a business relationship, but when he demands payment upfront for their meth, Tuco responds with brutal violence. Tuco severely beats Jesse and steals their product without paying, leaving Jesse bloodied and empty-handed. Walt decides to confront Tuco directly at his hideout, taking matters into his own hands. Using his chemistry knowledge, Walt creates an explosion that demonstrates his capabilities and forces Tuco to pay for the stolen meth. Impressed by Walt's boldness and scientific skills, Tuco agrees to work with them as their distributor, establishing a dangerous but potentially lucrative partnership.
