Walt White and Jesse Pinkman encounter a critical supply shortage when they cannot procure enough pseudoephedrine pills for their methamphetamine production. Jesse, now living in the RV after losing his house, decides to put his property on the market to generate income. Walt realizes they need an alternative production method to meet their obligations to drug dealer Tuco Salamanca. The partners identify methylamine as a substitute precursor chemical that will allow them to cook larger quantities using a different process. Walt and Jesse break into a chemical supply warehouse and steal a large barrel of methylamine, successfully obtaining the materials they need for production. Using the new cooking method, they produce the four pounds of methamphetamine they promised to deliver to Tuco. Meanwhile, Skyler White organizes a baby shower to celebrate her pregnancy. During the celebration, her sister Marie Schrader presents an expensive diamond tiara as a gift for the unborn baby. Skyler feels uncomfortable with the extravagant present and decides to return it to the jewelry store. When Skyler attempts to return the tiara, store security detains her and reveals that the item was reported stolen. Confronted with this information, Skyler directly asks Marie about the tiara's origin. Marie flatly denies stealing the jewelry, creating tension between the sisters. Walt and Jesse arrive at their meeting point to deliver the completed methamphetamine to Tuco and his associates, No-Doze and Gonzo. During the transaction, No-Doze makes a casual comment to Walt about the business arrangement. Tuco interprets this as disrespectful interference and flies into a violent rage. He savagely beats No-Doze in front of Walt and Jesse, who watch in horror as their business partner demonstrates his unpredictable and dangerous nature. After the brutal assault, Tuco drives away, leaving Walt and Jesse shaken by the violent display and questioning their partnership with the volatile dealer.
