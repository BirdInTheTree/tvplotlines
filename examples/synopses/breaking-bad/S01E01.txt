Walter White celebrates his 50th birthday with his pregnant wife Skyler White and teenage son Walter Jr. before teaching chemistry at a local high school. During his second job at a car wash, Walter suffers severe coughing fits and collapses. At the hospital, doctors diagnose him with Stage 3 terminal lung cancer, giving him limited time to live. Walter's brother-in-law Hank Schrader, a DEA agent, invites him on a ride-along to a methamphetamine lab raid to cheer him up. During the bust, DEA agents arrest a cook named Emilio while Walter waits in the car. Walter spots his former student Jesse Pinkman escaping through a neighbor's window and realizes Jesse is involved in the drug trade. Facing financial ruin from medical bills and wanting to provide for his family after his death, Walter tracks down Jesse and proposes a partnership where Walter will cook methamphetamine using his chemistry expertise while Jesse handles sales and distribution. Jesse initially resists but agrees when Walter demonstrates his superior product quality. They drive to a remote desert location and begin cooking crystal meth in Jesse's recreational vehicle. Walter's advanced chemistry knowledge produces an exceptionally pure product that impresses Jesse. Jesse contacts Krazy-8, a local distributor and Emilio's cousin, to arrange a meeting for their first sale. When Jesse and Walter meet with Krazy-8 and Emilio in the desert, Emilio recognizes Walter from the DEA raid and realizes he is connected to law enforcement. Jesse panics and tells Walter to run, but accidentally knocks himself unconscious during the confrontation. Realizing that both dealers intend to kill them, Walter pretends to cooperate by offering to teach them his cooking method. Instead, he uses his chemistry knowledge to create phosphine gas, poisoning both Krazy-8 and Emilio before escaping with the unconscious Jesse.
