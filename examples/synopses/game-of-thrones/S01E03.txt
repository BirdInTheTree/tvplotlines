In King's Landing, Ned attends his first Small Council meeting and discovers the realm is deeply in debt and poorly managed. Learning Arya received a sword from Jon and aspires to fight, Ned arranges swordsmanship lessons for her. Catelyn secretly travels to King's Landing to warn Ned about the assassination attempt but is intercepted by childhood friend Petyr Baelish. At Winterfell, Bran learns he will never walk again and cannot remember his fall. At the Wall, Jon proves superior to the low-born Night's Watch recruits. Lord Commander Jeor Mormont asks departing Tyrion to request more recruits from the King. In Essos, pregnant Daenerys stands up to her abusive brother Viserys, threatening him if he harms her again.
