Ned Stark attends his first meeting of the King's Small Council in King's Landing, where he discovers that the realm is deeply in debt and has been poorly managed under King Robert's rule. The council discusses financial matters that reveal the crown's precarious position. After learning that his daughter Arya has received a sword from her half-brother Jon Snow and aspires to learn swordwork, Ned arranges for her to take private sword lessons with a Braavosi water dancing master. Meanwhile, Catelyn Stark travels secretly to King's Landing to warn her husband about the assassination attempt on their son Bran, but her arrival is intercepted by Petyr Baelish, known as Littlefinger, who was her childhood friend and now serves as a member of the Small Council. Littlefinger takes Catelyn to a brothel where they can speak privately about the danger to her family. At Winterfell, Bran Stark awakens from his coma but learns from Maester Luwin that he will never walk again due to his injuries from the fall. Despite his physical recovery, Bran remains unable to remember the circumstances that led to his accident, leaving the mystery of his fall unresolved. At the Wall, Jon Snow continues his training with the Night's Watch recruits, demonstrating superior fighting skills compared to the lowborn men and convicted criminals who have joined the order. His abilities create tension with his fellow recruits who resent his noble upbringing and training. Lord Commander Jeor Mormont recognizes Jon's potential and asks the departing Tyrion Lannister to speak with King Robert about sending more recruits to strengthen the Night's Watch, as their numbers have dwindled dangerously low. Across the Narrow Sea, Daenerys Targaryen has grown more confident in her role as Khaleesi and is now pregnant with Khal Drogo's child. When her brother Viserys threatens and abuses her as he has done throughout their lives, she stands up to him for the first time, warning him that if he ever strikes her again, she will have him killed, marking a significant shift in their power dynamic.
