North of the Seven Kingdoms, Night's Watch soldiers encounter supernatural White Walkers who kill all but one man. The lone survivor flees south but is captured near Winterfell and brought before Eddard "Ned" Stark, Warden of the North. Ned executes the deserter for abandoning his post, as duty demands. While returning from the execution, the Stark family discovers six orphaned direwolf pups, and Ned allows each of his children, including his bastard son Jon Snow, to adopt one. In King's Landing, Jon Arryn, the Hand of the King and the realm's most powerful advisor, dies suddenly under mysterious circumstances. King Robert Baratheon, Ned's longtime friend and former war companion, decides to travel north to Winterfell with his court. Robert arrives at Winterfell and asks Ned to become his new Hand of the King, while also proposing to betroth his heir Prince Joffrey to Ned's eldest daughter Sansa Stark. The political marriage would strengthen the alliance between their houses. Catelyn Stark receives a secret letter from her sister Lysa Arryn, Jon Arryn's widow, claiming that the Lannister family murdered Jon Arryn. Catelyn fears the Lannisters are now plotting against King Robert himself. While the adults negotiate, Ned's young son Bran Stark climbs the walls of Winterfell's tallest tower, despite repeated warnings about the danger. At the tower's top, Bran witnesses Queen Cersei Lannister and her twin brother Jaime Lannister engaged in sexual intercourse. When Jaime realizes they have been discovered, he pushes Bran from the window to protect their secret, as their incestuous relationship would destroy the royal family if revealed. Across the Narrow Sea in the continent of Essos, exiled Prince Viserys Targaryen arranges for his younger sister Daenerys to marry Khal Drogo, a powerful Dothraki warlord. Viserys believes this alliance will provide him with the army he needs to invade Westeros and reclaim the Iron Throne that was taken from their family. At the wedding ceremony, Daenerys receives three ancient dragon eggs as a gift, petrified remnants of the creatures that once gave House Targaryen dominion over the Seven Kingdoms.
