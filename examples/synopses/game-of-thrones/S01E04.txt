Tyrion stops at Winterfell while returning south and gives Bran a saddle design allowing him to ride despite his paralysis. Catelyn encounters Tyrion at a roadside inn and rallies her father's bannermen to arrest him for allegedly conspiring to murder Bran. In King's Landing, Ned secretly investigates Jon Arryn's death and discovers Gendry, one of King Robert's bastard children. At the Wall, Jon protects awkward recruit Samwell Tarly from abuse by other Night's Watchmen. In Essos, Viserys grows increasingly frustrated with his newly empowered sister Daenerys and clashes with her.
