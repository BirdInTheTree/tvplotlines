Major league pitcher Hank Wiggen suffers a severely broken arm that reveals bizarre bone loss, ending his comeback plans. House suspects Hank is lying about steroid use given his history of drug abuse, especially as his condition deteriorates. When Hank's kidneys begin failing, his wife offers to donate one of hers, but she would have to abort her early pregnancy, which Hank opposes. House discovers that Hank's wife has lost her sense of smell, indicating both have smoked cannabis grown on cadmium-polluted soil, causing cadmium poisoning. Meanwhile, Foreman dates a pharmaceutical representative and House attends a monster truck rally with Cameron.
