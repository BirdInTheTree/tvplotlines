House overhears a conversation about a sick baby and investigates the maternity ward, predicting an epidemic. After confirming the severity of the outbreak, Cuddy quarantines the entire maternity ward. House begins treating the affected children, but when two babies develop kidney failure, he must test which drug caused the complication, resulting in one infant's death. An autopsy reveals the presence of echovirus 11, CMV, and parvovirus B19 antibodies in the deceased baby. The team tests the mothers and identifies echovirus as the epidemic's cause. Using an experimental antiviral treatment, they successfully cure the remaining babies. Determined to find the virus's entry point, House discovers an elderly hospital volunteer who has been coughing and wiping her nose while distributing toys and blankets throughout the ward, identifying her as the source of contamination.
