A young boy believes he will die after consulting a Ouija board and is hospitalized with pneumonia. Chase's estranged father arrives at the hospital and assists House's team with the diagnosis. House becomes suspicious of the seemingly random visit and questions Chase's father, who reveals he has terminal cancer. The team diagnoses the boy with both anthrax and leprosy. Chase and his father briefly reconcile before his father departs for the airport, leaving their relationship somewhat resolved despite the terminal diagnosis.
