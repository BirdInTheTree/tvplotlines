House fights a meningitis outbreak and identifies a young patient who doesn't fit the typical criteria, giving his team one hour to produce results. House attempts to convince Cameron to return following Vogler's departure, but she demands he explain why he really wants her back. House and Wilson interview potential replacements for Cameron's position. The young patient is diagnosed with thrombotic thrombocytopenic purpura secondary to pregnancy. Cameron agrees to return to the team in exchange for House taking her on a dinner date.
