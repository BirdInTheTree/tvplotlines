House is court-ordered to diagnose mobster Joey Arnello, who is scheduled for federal testimony and entry into Witness Protection. Joey's brother, a lawyer, works against the medical team and the testimony when Joey is diagnosed with Hepatitis C. The team ultimately discovers Joey has ornithine transcarbamylase deficiency. Meanwhile, Cuddy continues battling Vogler over House's value to the hospital, with Vogler increasingly determined to eliminate House's department.
