Ed returns from jogging with his best friend to find his bedridden wife Elise behaving uncharacteristically abusive toward him. House concludes she either has rabbit fever from cooking rabbits or African sleeping sickness, which would require sexual transmission since neither has traveled to Africa. Both Ed and Elise vehemently deny having affairs, so House begins treating Elise for rabbit fever. When this treatment fails, House successfully treats her for sleeping sickness. Under the pressure of her diagnosis and treatment, Elise confesses to having an affair with her husband's best friend, causing Ed to leave her despite her recovery.
