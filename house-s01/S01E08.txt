House investigates the mysterious poisoning of high school student Matt Davis. When another teenager arrives with identical symptoms but no apparent connection to Matt, House must find the common link between the cases. The team discovers both teens purchased inexpensive jeans at a car boot sale, and these garments were exposed to high doses of pesticides, causing phosdrin poisoning. Meanwhile, House treats an elderly woman at the clinic who has been flirting with him, ultimately diagnosing her with neurosyphilis.
