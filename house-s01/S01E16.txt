House treats an overweight ten-year-old girl who suffers a heart attack, while her mother insists the team look beyond her weight for the real diagnosis. Adding pressure to the case, Vogler demands House fire a team member. House discovers that Chase has been feeding information about his cases to Vogler. When House tells Cuddy and Vogler he chooses to fire Chase, Vogler rejects this decision and demands House fire either Foreman or Cameron instead. The team diagnoses the girl with Cushing's disease, explaining both her weight and cardiac symptoms.
