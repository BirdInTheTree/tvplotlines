House's ex-girlfriend Stacy Warner visits seeking help for her husband Mark's medical condition. Meanwhile, Cuddy forces House to lecture medical students on patient diagnosis, presenting three scenarios involving different causes of leg pain. House uses the teaching opportunity to explore cases including a farmer with streptococcal infection, a volleyball player with osteosarcoma, and his own past case of thigh muscle infarction. The lecture also covers a case of lead paint poisoning in Professor Riley. Through the teaching exercise, House reflects on his diagnostic methodology while dealing with Stacy's unexpected return to his life.
